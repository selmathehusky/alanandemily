What's a Meadery?

From flower to flute. Mead is a fermented beverage made from honey and is one of the oldest drinks produced in ancient Europe, Africa, and Asia. The Heidrun Meadery is reviving this drink in an innovative way, producing it in traditional French Méthode Champenoise.

Emily and I have been coming to the Heidrun Meadery since we first started dating. We fell in love with the space and how mead brings you closer to amazing honey bees. We would often sit on the patio watching the sunset while talking and dreaming about our future. We invite you to share in our wonderful experiences here!

Attire

Dress code is casual/semi-formal. Guys, please no jeans, a jacket or blazer would be great with tie optional. Girls, cocktail or summer evening dresses are great. Note, it may get chilly in the evening.

The ceremony and cocktail hour will be held outdoors. Please note there will likely be a short walk through unpaved and grassy areas to the ceremony location. The reception will be held indoors in the greenhouse.

Temperatures in Point Reyes in August are pleasant, with highs of approximately 75℉ and lows of 56℉.

Locations
